ARTIST INFO

Maddi Red



Maddi is a tattoo artist with over 10 years of experience. Her specialty is black and gray tattoos, and she's known for her intricate and detailed designs. She loves creating custom tattoos that tell a story and reflect her clients' personalities.

Alex Blue



Alex is a versatile tattoo artist with a passion for color and bold lines. He's been in the industry for 8 years and has experience in a wide range of styles, from traditional to neo-traditional to Japanese. He enjoys collaborating to create tattoos that are unique and meaningful.

Russ Green



Russ is a self-taught tattoo artist who's been tattooing for 5 years. He specializes in fine line tattoos and minimalist designs. He's inspired by nature and he loves creating tattoos that are both elegant and timeless.
